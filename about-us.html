About Us

It is an unfortunate reality that budgets in the education sector are under enormous pressure. Equipment has to last for longer or sit on a waiting list as funds are stretched thinner and thinner. To address this challenge, we have built Reuse4education where education institutions – universities, colleges and schools – can help one another re-use, recycle, upcycle, and share vital equipment.

Reuse4education is an online platform that’s designed to support our community of schools, colleges and universities, giving them a space where they can get rid of unwanted items, find kit they need, loan equipment to one another, and provide much needed help and support. Our global supply chain network will help you find what you need whether you want to donate, sell, swap or lend.

With us, you can help other education institutions while reusing and recycling items you no longer want. Not only will this help you save money, but it supports the environment and the global need to reduce waste. You reduce your waste disposal and purchasing costs.

Reus4education focus is exclusively on education establishments as our community is designed to support your specialised requirements.

The premise is simple:

  •    You have equipment you no longer need, be it science apparatus or PE kit
  •    You list the equipment on our site so another school can make use of it – you opt in to donate, sell or swap.
  •    You need to borrow equipment for a specific event.
  •    You list your needs and another school, college or university may be able to assist you.

Everything from classroom desks to science kit to PE equipment to furniture and textbooks is welcome.

Save money. Save space. Save your budget.
